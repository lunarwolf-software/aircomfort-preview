# Website Opportunity — Aircomfort Airconditioning

**Internal document for LunarWolf Software. Not for public website visitors.**
Based on the LunarWolf Business Discovery Report (July 2026).

## Current digital position

- **No website** — the Google Business Profile shows Google's "Add website" prompt.
- **No established visual identity** — no logo, colours or brand assets found anywhere.
- **No public reviews** — zero Google reviews; no presence on Hellopeter, Snupit, Kandua or similar.
- **Limited online footprint** — the verified presence is the Google Business Profile plus a Procompare lead-generation profile (which displays competitors alongside the business and charges for leads).
- **Limited lead-capture options** — customers can only phone; there is no form, no WhatsApp entry point, no email, and nothing to build trust before the call.
- **Listing hygiene issues** — two different phone numbers circulating in directories, and third-party photos (including a competitor-watermarked image) attached to the Google listing.

## Website opportunity

A professional site can help with — stated as opportunities, not promised outcomes:

- **Credibility** — customers deciding between local installers currently see competitors with websites and reviews; Aircomfort has neither. A clean site closes the most visible trust gap.
- **Customer enquiries** — every page funnels to call/WhatsApp, converting interest into conversations.
- **Mobile contact** — most local-service searches happen on phones; the sticky call/WhatsApp bar makes contact one tap away.
- **Service explanation** — installation vs supply vs servicing vs portable AC is explained clearly, reducing unqualified calls.
- **Local search visibility** — location-relevant pages, clean structure and LocalBusiness schema give Google something to rank beyond the bare listing.
- **Quote requests** — the WhatsApp enquiry generator collects suburb + service + photos, producing better-qualified first conversations.
- **Gallery presentation** — a home for real installation photos once the owner supplies them.
- **Future review display** — a reviews-ready page that can showcase genuine Google reviews as they arrive.

## Customer journey

```text
Google Search
→ Google Business Profile
→ Website
→ Services or Gallery
→ Call or WhatsApp
→ Quote conversation
```

## Competitive position

Local competitors (Supreme Air-Conditioning, Aircon Master, Airberg, Mr Aircon) have functional but dated template sites. Aircomfort can stand out through:

- cleaner, faster design than any local competitor site;
- simpler contact — one tap to call or WhatsApp from anywhere on the site;
- honest service presentation with no inflated claims (a real differentiator in this market);
- a stronger mobile experience;
- real future installation photos with suburb-level captions;
- genuine future reviews displayed prominently once earned;
- useful FAQs that answer what customers actually ask;
- local search pages (service areas) competitors mostly lack.
